Reproducible public-data reanalysis of a lung adenocarcinoma single-cell atlas, focusing on tumor microenvironment composition, curated expression signatures, cell-subtype context, and paired-patient sensitivity checks.

## What To Highlight

- Public GEO dataset with 208,506 cells and 58 samples.
- Memory-aware raw UMI signature scoring.
- Sample-level and paired-patient analyses rather than only pooled cell-level plots.
- Committed preview figures/tables plus validation checks.
- Clear limitations around public annotations and exploratory signature scoring.
